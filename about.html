Since 1945

A Family Legacy of Electrical Craftsmanship

It started with one man, a truck, and a simple promise: do quality work, treat people right, and stand behind what you build.

Founded in 1945, Riverside Electric Inc. began as a small family electrical shop serving the Cincinnati area. Through four generations, we've grown into a full-service electrical contractor with three locations across Ohio, Kentucky, and Indiana — but our core values haven't changed.

We've weathered eight decades of industry change: from knob-and-tube wiring to smart panels, from incandescent bulbs to LED retrofits, from no generators to whole-home standby systems. Through it all, our master electricians have stayed at the forefront of the trade, bringing expertise and integrity to every project.

Today, as a Generac PowerPro Premier Dealer and BBB A+ Accredited Business, we serve commercial, industrial, and residential clients throughout the tri-state region — with the same family values that started it all.

Our Journey

80 Years of Milestones

From a one-truck operation to a tri-state electrical leader.

1945
The Beginning
Riverside Electric is founded in Cincinnati with a single truck and a commitment to honest, quality electrical work.
1960s
Growing With Cincinnati
The second generation joins the business. We expand into commercial electrical work and lighting contracts across the region.
1980s
Expanding Services
We add lighting management programs and preventive maintenance contracts, becoming a full-service electrical partner for businesses.
2000s
Generac Partnership
We become a Generac Authorized Dealer, bringing reliable standby power solutions to homes and businesses across the tri-state.
2010s
Three Locations, One Family
Columbus and Indianapolis offices open. The third and fourth generations take the helm, modernizing operations while preserving family values.
Today
PowerPro Premier & Beyond
We earn Generac's highest PowerPro Premier designation, add EV charging and LED retrofit services, and continue serving the tri-state with 24/7 emergency response — 80 years strong.
